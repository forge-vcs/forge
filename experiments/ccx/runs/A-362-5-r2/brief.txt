                   "proposal_revision_id": null|"...",
                   "decision_id": null|"...", "actor": null|"...",
                   "authored_time": null|ms } ] }

  commit_id). No ledger enrichment yet (362-4 adds it): provenance fields
  here come straight from the CommitObject via the provenance module.

  - Errors from the provenance module surface as typed envelope errors,
